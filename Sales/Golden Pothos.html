Golden Pothos

6.99

Golden Pothos are a beautiful, low-maintenance vine that is perfect for draping over a bookshelf or mantle. They are also known to be great for people with allergies and can help to remove toxins from the air in your home. They can be grown in pots or in hanging baskets. Golden Pothos are also known as money plants and said to bring good luck and wealth. They are perfect for adding a touch of greenery to any room.

Growth Habit

Perennial

Plant Use

Ornamental

Preferred Climate

Tropical climate

Light Requirements

Bright indirect light

Soil Need

Well-draining soil

Way of Pollination

Self pollinated
